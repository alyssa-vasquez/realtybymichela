About

Michela Reouveni understands that buying and selling real estate is an emotional as well as financial endeavor for every client. Having earned a degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in real estate, this is something Michela Reouveni takes to heart. Taking time to identify and meet each client's individual needs is central to her real estate philosophy. She takes deep pride in offering unparalleled service, and enjoys meeting and working with her clients through every stage of what can be a time consuming and challenging process.

Having traveled extensively, and being born in Africa, Michela Reouveni enjoys meeting and working with international clients, many of whom bring their cultural perspective on architecture and business to the table. She worked under and was mentored by Los Angeles' top agent Joe Babajan for eight years. Michela is an experienced agent with a network in the industry, who knows how to guide her clients into making the best possible deal for them, including all of the background details. She has assisted and partnered in various projects, many of them very complex and which required creative solutions, from Bel Air, Beverly Hills and the Hollywood Hills to the premiere West side of Los Angeles. Michela Reouveni is well known for her attention to detail, energy and competence, and for being readily available to her clients.
